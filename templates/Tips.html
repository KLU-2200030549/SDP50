Eat a Balanced Diet

Focus on consuming a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Limit processed foods, sugary snacks, and excessive intake of saturated and trans fats.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your body hydrated. Limit intake of sugary drinks and alcohol.

Regular Exercise

Engage in moderate exercise such as walking, swimming, or prenatal yoga, as long as your healthcare provider approves. Regular physical activity can help improve circulation, reduce stress, and promote overall well-being during pregnancy.

Last updated 59 mins ago

Manage Stress

Pregnancy can be a stressful time, so practice stress-reduction techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or prenatal massage. Surround yourself with supportive friends and family members who can help alleviate stress.
